District Team Leader at ICT Olympiad Bangladesh, Netrakona (2022) [Profile]

ICT Olympiad Bangladesh (ICTOB) is a significant annual competition in Bangladesh, spanning pre-school to university levels, and focusing on information and communications technology. It covers various topics, including ICT Fundamentals, Communication System and Networking, Number System and Digital Devices, Web Design and HTML, Basic Programming, and Database Management System, benefiting students preparing for Digital Bangladesh.
